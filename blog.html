Warranty Terms


Products can be returned in their original condition within 10 from the day of receipt if the order was shipped by a common carrier *. Products purchased and picked up directly from our office can be returned within 5 days from the invoice date. Return authorization is required. Products returned without a valid RMA number will not be accepted. Customer is responsible for all return shipping costs, customs duties and import taxes. Warranty coverage is limited to electrical failures, which occur when equipment is operated according to manufacturer's instructions. Removing or tampering with warranty seals or attempting to upgrade firmware will result in forfeiture of the 10 day right of return and cancellation of Equiptek Labs warranty. At our discretion warranty remedy shall be limited to repair, replacement or refund of the purchase price.
‍
* Following items do not qualify for the 10 day right of return privilege: New instruments and accessories, software, device programmers, certain instruments that require software on magnetic media for normal operation, internal replacement parts.

How to place a credit card order


You can place a credit card order by calling 650-261-1711 from 8:30 to 6 Pacific standard time ( GMT-8 hours ) or by requesting an invoice and paying with a credit card at our secure credit card processor website. Local customers are welcome to pay on line and pick up in person at our office.

‍The following information is required for a credit card order:
1. Name of the person placing an order along with a phone number and email address
‍
2. Shipping address for the order ( we do not ship to the PO box addresses)
‍
3. Billing address ( The address where you receive your credit card statements)

4. Shipping method (we do not use customers or 3rd party shipping accounts. Prepaid return shipping labels are acceptable)
‍
5. International customers may need to supply additional information

Sales Tax and Customs Duties


Equiptek Labs does not collect sales taxes or customs duties on orders shipped to locations outside of the state of California. For shipments to San Mateo County addresses, local state sales tax will be added to the order total. For all other California counties sales tax rate is 7.25%. If you are exempt from the California sales tax please fax or email your Resale permit and completed Blanket Certificate of Resale form to either : 650-284-2133 or sales@equiptek.com
